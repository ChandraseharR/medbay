Other names: varicella
Specialty: Infectious disease
Symptoms: Small, itchy blisters,headache,loss of appetite,tiredness,fever[1]
Usual onset: 10–21 days after exposure[2]
Duration: 5–10 days[1]
Causes: Varicella zoster virus[3]
Prevention: Varicella vaccine[4]
Medication: Calamine lotion,paracetamol(acetaminophen),aciclovir[5]
Deaths: 6,400 per year (with shingles)[6]
